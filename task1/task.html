COMPUTER TECHNOLOGIES

The demand for backend development is growing exponentially due to its massive contribution to developing seamless and functional websites. In fact, according to research conducted by the US Bureau of Labor and Statistics, the demand for backend development will increase tremendously by 2028 due to the surge in demand for backend developers in the eCommerce industry. Full-stack developers have a high level of competency in both frontend and backend languages. They are skilled in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, NodeJS, ExpressJS, Django, and PHP. Equally, full-stack developers understand various database management systems (DBMS) like MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and more.
